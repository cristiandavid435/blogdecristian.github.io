Critical thinking

With news available at the tap of a finger, keyboard, or remote, we are often exposed to a barrage of news media. Some of it is high quality, informational news, while other pieces may be riddled with biases, inaccuracies, and misinformation. That’s why it’s so important for students to learn to properly evaluate the news and content they’re consuming. We live in the age of information, but also in the age of misinformation. Never before have we had instant access to so much data, news, and opinions.
